Wedding collections include a complimentary mini engagement session so that we can get to know each other. The session is optional but I encourage couples to schedule one since it is great practice for your wedding day! Sessions last up to 30-45 minutes and are scheduled on Sundays at sunrise or sunset.

online
Final images are fully edited and will be delivered via an online gallery. Images can be downloaded from the gallery and saved to any computer or mobile device. I do not watermark the images and the number of image delivered is subjective, below is an estimate based on hourly coverage booked.
